Principles for Respectful Encounters

Approach every interaction with humility, curiosity, and consent. Your presence should leave a positive, lasting impression.

Etiquette

  • Ask before taking photos
  • Use greetings in local languages
  • Be mindful of religious customs

Gifting

  • Avoid giving money directly to children
  • Support community projects via trusted partners
  • Buy locally made crafts at fair prices

Dress & Conduct

Dress modestly in rural areas and during cultural visits. Follow your guide's advice on appropriate behavior in sacred spaces.

Note: Always seek consent for photos and recordings.

Experience Community-Led Tourism

Join activities that directly benefit local households and cultural centers.
