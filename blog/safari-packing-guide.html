Safari Packing Guide

Pack smart, travel light, and enjoy every moment in the wild.

May 2025 • 6 min read • Trip Planner
  1. Home
  2. Travel Tips
  3. Packing Guide

Essential Checklist

Weather and terrain vary—layer up and keep it neutral to blend in with the environment.

Clothing

  • Neutral colors (khaki, olive, brown)
  • Lightweight long sleeves and trousers
  • Warm layer for early mornings
  • Rain jacket (especially Mar–May, Sep–Nov)

Gear & Essentials

  • Binoculars, power bank, universal adapter
  • Sun protection: hat, sunglasses, SPF
  • Personal medication & basic first aid
  • Reusable water bottle

Documents & Money

  • Passport, visas, yellow fever card
  • Travel insurance details
  • USD cash (newer notes) and cards
