Travel lighter, support conservation, and empower communities.

Make Every Choice Count

Small decisions add up. From what you pack to where you stay, your choices directly influence wildlife, habitats, and livelihoods.

Reduce Waste

  • Carry a reusable water bottle and filter
  • Avoid single-use plastics (bags, straws)
  • Choose reef/river-safe toiletries

Support Conservation

  • Pick lodges with certified eco-practices
  • Book tours that fund park protection
  • Respect park rules and buffer zones

Community-Led Tourism

Choose experiences that are owned or led by local communities. This ensures tourism revenue supports education, healthcare, and cultural preservation.

Tip: Ask operators how your booking supports conservation and local livelihoods.

Ethical Wildlife Viewing

  • Never feed or touch wildlife
  • Keep noise low and maintain distance
  • Follow your guide’s instructions at all times
