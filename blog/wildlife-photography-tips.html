Techniques With Care

Great images start with empathy. Let behavior guide your shot, not the other way around.

Gear & Settings

  • Telephoto lens (300mm+ preferred)
  • High ISO, fast shutter in low light
  • Use silent/electronic shutter if available

Ethics

  • No baiting or distressing animals
  • Keep a respectful distance
  • Follow your guide and park rules

Storytelling Over Close-ups

Include environment, interactions, and behaviors. Your images can inspire conservation when they tell the bigger story.
