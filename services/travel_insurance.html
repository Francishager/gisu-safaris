Simple Claims Process

Easy steps to file and track claims with our partner insurers

1. Contact Insurer Assistance

Use the insurer's 24/7 emergency assistance line listed in your policy documents. Gisu Safaris can help you locate the correct contact if needed.

2. Submit Documents to Insurer

Provide required forms, receipts, and reports via the insurer’s claims portal or as instructed in your policy. We can furnish trip confirmations upon request.

3. Insurer Claim Review

The insurer assesses your claim and may request additional information. Gisu Safaris supports with relevant travel documentation where applicable.

4. Reimbursement by Insurer

Approved claims are paid directly by the insurer via their designated payment methods and timelines.
