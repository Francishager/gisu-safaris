Rwanda Gorilla Trekking Safari

Experience the magic of encountering endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat within Rwanda's pristine Volcanoes National Park. A once-in-a-lifetime adventure combining wildlife, culture, and conservation.

View Itinerary Book Now

Experience Rwanda's Mountain Gorillas

Gorilla Permits Included

All gorilla trekking permits ($1,500 value per person) are included in your package, ensuring guaranteed access to mountain gorillas.

Rwanda's Volcanoes National Park is home to several habituated mountain gorilla families, offering visitors the extraordinary opportunity to observe these magnificent creatures up close. Our expertly crafted 4-day safari combines gorilla trekking with cultural experiences and scenic beauty of the Land of a Thousand Hills.

Conservation Impact

Your visit directly contributes to mountain gorilla conservation and supports local communities through tourism revenue.

Photography Friendly

Capture incredible moments with these gentle giants in their natural habitat (flash photography not permitted).

Mountain Gorilla Facts

  • Only ~1,000 mountain gorillas remain worldwide
  • Rwanda hosts 1/3 of the global population
  • Gorillas share 98% of human DNA
  • Family groups are led by a silverback male
  • Adult males can weigh up to 200kg
  • Primarily herbivorous diet
  • Highly intelligent and emotional beings
  • Live in montane and bamboo forests
