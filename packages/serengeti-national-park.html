Great Migration Calendar

Plan your visit to witness nature's greatest spectacle

December - March
Calving Season

Southern Serengeti & Ndutu area. Over 500,000 wildebeest calves are born during this period. Incredible predator action as lions, cheetahs, and hyenas take advantage of abundant prey.

Best for: Newborn wildlife, predator action, fewer crowds
April - June
Northern Movement

Migration moves through Central and Western Serengeti. Herds begin their journey north in search of fresh grazing. Great opportunities for river crossings at Grumeti River.

Best for: River crossings, central Serengeti, mating rituals
July - October
Mara River Drama

Northern Serengeti - the most famous migration period. Dramatic Mara River crossings with crocodiles waiting. Peak season with the highest wildlife concentrations.

Best for: Mara River crossings, highest concentrations, peak season
November
Southern Return

Short rains begin and herds start moving south through Eastern and Central Serengeti. Excellent game viewing with fewer crowds as animals disperse across the plains.

Best for: Fewer crowds, good wildlife, transitional period
Migration Planning Tips
Best Photography:

July-October for river crossings, December-March for calving and predator action

Fewer Crowds:

April-May and November offer excellent wildlife with fewer tourists

Weather Considerations:

June-October is dry season, November-May includes rainy periods

Advanced Booking:

Peak season (July-October) requires 6-12 months advance booking
