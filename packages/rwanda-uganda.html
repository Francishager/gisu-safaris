Overview

Follow the path of mountain gorillas across borders for an unforgettable conservation story and close wildlife encounters.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1 Kigali Arrival
Transfer to Volcanoes National Park

City highlights en route and lodge check-in near the Virunga volcanoes.

Day 2 Volcanoes NP
Gorilla Trekking in Rwanda

Briefing at Kinigi; trek to meet a gorilla family in lush montane forest.

Day 3 Cross to Uganda
Drive to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Border crossing and scenic transfer to Bwindi; evening at leisure.

Day 4 Bwindi
Second Gorilla Tracking

Another gorilla encounter on the Ugandan side for diverse experiences.

Day 5 Mgahinga/Volcanoes
Golden Monkey Tracking

Track playful golden monkeys in bamboo zones along the Virunga slopes.

Day 6 Kibale & Departure
Chimpanzee Trek or Kigali Exit

Optional chimp trek in Kibale or return to Kigali for your outbound flight.
