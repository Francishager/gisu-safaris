Overview

From gorilla tracking to the Great Migration, this grand tour brings together East Africa's most iconic experiences in a seamless journey.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1 Entebbe/Kampala
Arrival and Transfer to Bwindi

Welcome to Uganda. Scenic drive or flight to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Day 2 Bwindi
Gorilla Trekking Adventure

Guided trek to meet a mountain gorilla family with ranger support.

Day 3 Kenya
Fly to Masai Mara

Transfer via Nairobi; sunset game drive across the golden plains.

Day 4 Masai Mara
Great Migration or Big Five Safari

Full-day exploration in prime wildlife areas with picnic lunch.

Day 5 Tanzania
Serengeti to Ngorongoro

Track predators and herds, then continue to the Ngorongoro highlands.

Day 6 Rwanda
Kigali City & Departure

Insightful city tour and cultural highlights before your flight home.
