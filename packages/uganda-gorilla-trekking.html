Day-by-Day Itinerary

Your complete 5-day gorilla trekking adventure

1
Day 1
Arrival

Arrival in Kampala & Transfer to Bwindi

Upon arrival at Entebbe International Airport, you'll be warmly welcomed by your professional safari guide. Begin your scenic 8-hour drive to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, enjoying spectacular views of Uganda's rolling hills, terraced farmlands, and local villages. Stop for lunch in Mbarara town and arrive at your luxury eco-lodge in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.

Lunch, Dinner
Luxury Eco-Lodge
2
Day 2
Gorilla Trek

Mountain Gorilla Trekking Experience

Early morning briefing at the park headquarters before beginning your gorilla trekking adventure. Led by experienced rangers, trek through the dense forest for 2-6 hours (depending on gorilla location) to find a habituated gorilla family. Spend an unforgettable hour observing these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat - watching them feed, play, and interact. Return to the lodge for a well-deserved rest and celebration dinner.

Gorilla Permits: Valid permits worth $700 per person are included in your package.
All Meals
Luxury Eco-Lodge
3
Day 3
Culture

Batwa Cultural Experience & Community Walk

After breakfast, visit the Batwa (Pygmy) community to learn about their traditional forest lifestyle before conservation efforts relocated them. Experience their ancient hunting techniques, traditional dances, and forest knowledge. Take a guided community walk through local villages, visit schools and health centers to understand community conservation efforts. Optional visit to local craft centers to purchase authentic souvenirs.

All Meals
Luxury Eco-Lodge
4
Day 4
Optional

Second Gorilla Trek or Forest Walk

Choose your adventure: embark on a second gorilla trekking experience to visit a different gorilla family (additional permit required - $700), or enjoy a peaceful nature walk through Bwindi Forest with bird watching opportunities. The forest is home to over 350 bird species including 23 Albertine Rift endemics. Alternatively, relax at your lodge and enjoy the tranquil forest surroundings with spa treatments available.

Optional: Second gorilla permit available for $700 (subject to availability).
All Meals
Luxury Eco-Lodge
5
Day 5
Departure

Return Journey to Kampala

After breakfast and final moments enjoying the forest atmosphere, begin your return journey to Kampala. Stop at the Uganda Equator for photos and souvenir shopping at local craft shops. Continue to Kampala with lunch en route, arriving in the evening for onward transfer to Entebbe Airport for your departure flight or additional nights in Kampala if extending your stay.

Breakfast, Lunch
Airport Transfer
