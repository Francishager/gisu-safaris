Day-by-Day Experience

Your complete 7-day luxury beach resort adventure

1
Day 1
Arrival & Welcome

Arrival at Neptune Paradise Resort

Arrive at Moi International Airport Mombasa where you'll be warmly welcomed by our representative. Enjoy a comfortable transfer to Neptune Paradise Resort along the scenic coastal road. Check into your ocean-view suite and receive a refreshing welcome drink. Spend the afternoon at leisure exploring the resort's facilities, relaxing by the infinity pool, or taking your first stroll on the pristine beach. Evening welcome dinner featuring fresh seafood and local Swahili specialties.

Welcome Drink, Dinner
Ocean View Suite
2
Day 2
Beach & Water Sports

Beach Activities & Water Sports Adventure

Start your day with a sumptuous breakfast buffet featuring international and local cuisine. Spend the morning on the beach with complimentary water sports activities - try snorkeling in the coral gardens, kayaking along the coast, or windsurfing on the gentle waves. Afternoon at the resort's spa for rejuvenating treatments (optional) or continue beach relaxation. Evening sundowner drinks followed by a beachfront barbecue dinner under the stars.

Water Sports: All non-motorized water sports are complimentary including snorkeling gear, kayaks, and windsurfing equipment.
All Meals
Water Sports Included
3
Day 3
Cultural Exploration

Mombasa Old Town & Fort Jesus Cultural Tour

After breakfast, embark on a fascinating cultural tour to historic Mombasa Old Town. Explore the narrow streets filled with Swahili architecture, visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site Fort Jesus, and discover the rich history of this ancient trading port. Browse local markets for spices, fabrics, and handcrafted souvenirs. Lunch at a traditional Swahili restaurant. Return to the resort for an afternoon at leisure by the pool or beach. Evening themed dinner with live traditional music and dance performances.

All Meals
Cultural Tours Included
4
Day 4
Marine Adventure

Wasini Island & Dolphin Watching Safari

Early morning departure for an exciting marine adventure to Wasini Island. Board a traditional dhow boat for dolphin watching in the Shimoni Channel - spot bottlenose and humpback dolphins in their natural habitat. Continue to Wasini Island for snorkeling in the pristine coral gardens of Kisite-Mpunguti Marine National Park. Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch on the island prepared by local chefs. Explore the island's mangrove forests and learn about marine conservation efforts. Return to the resort by evening.

Marine Park: Snorkeling in protected coral reefs with over 250 fish species and pristine marine ecosystems.
All Meals
Dhow Boat Safari
5
Day 5
Relaxation & Wellness

Spa Day & Beach Relaxation

Indulge in a day of ultimate relaxation and wellness. Start with a morning yoga session on the beach followed by a healthy breakfast. Spend time at the resort's world-class spa enjoying massage treatments, facials, and body wraps using natural local ingredients. Relax by the infinity pool or private beach cabanas. Optional activities include beach volleyball, beach tennis, or simply lounging with a good book. Sunset cocktails followed by a romantic dinner at the beachfront restaurant.

All Meals
Spa Services Available
6
Day 6
Adventure Options

Optional Excursions or Resort Activities

Choose your adventure: Option 1 - Deep sea fishing expedition for marlin, sailfish, and other game fish. Option 2 - Visit to Shimba Hills National Reserve for elephant viewing and scenic landscapes. Option 3 - Relax at the resort with access to all facilities including tennis court, fitness center, kids' club, and multiple swimming pools. Evening features the resort's weekly cultural show with traditional Swahili music, dance, and storytelling followed by a farewell dinner.

Optional Excursions: Additional activities available at extra cost - deep sea fishing ($150pp), Shimba Hills safari ($80pp).
All Meals
Optional Activities

Day 7
Departure

Departure Day

Enjoy a leisurely final breakfast at the resort with ocean views. Take last moments to soak up the sun, capture final photos, or purchase souvenirs from the resort boutique. Late check-out available until 2 PM. Transfer to Moi International Airport for your onward journey or extend your stay in Kenya. Departure with unforgettable memories of Kenya's stunning coastline, warm hospitality, and the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure that Neptune Paradise Resort offers.
