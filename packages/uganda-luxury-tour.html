Your 8-Day Luxury Itinerary

A seamless journey through Uganda's highlights

Day 1: Arrival in Entebbe

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport, where you'll be met by our VIP guide and transferred to the serene Lake Victoria Serena Golf Resort & Spa.

Day 2: Fly to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Take a morning flight to Kihihi, followed by a scenic drive to the luxurious Bwindi Lodge, offering stunning forest views.

Day 3: Ultimate Gorilla Trekking

An unforgettable day tracking mountain gorillas. A challenging yet immensely rewarding experience. Enjoy post-trek massages at the lodge.

Day 4: Fly to Murchison Falls

Fly to Murchison Falls National Park. Check into the opulent Nile Safari Lodge, overlooking the majestic River Nile.

Day 5: Private Game Drive & Nile Cruise

Morning game drive to spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more. In the afternoon, take a luxury boat cruise to the base of Murchison Falls.

Day 6: Delta Boat Trip & Sundowners

Explore the Nile Delta by boat for a chance to see the rare Shoebill Stork. Enjoy evening sundowners with panoramic views.

Day 7: Relaxation or Chimpanzee Trekking

A day of leisure at the lodge or an optional chimpanzee trek in Budongo Forest. Your choice.

Day 8: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast before flying from Murchison back to Entebbe for your international departure.
