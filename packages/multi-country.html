Everything you need to know about multi-country safaris

We assist with all visa applications and provide guidance on requirements for each country. Most nationalities can get visas on arrival or apply online. We handle all the paperwork and ensure you have the right documentation for seamless border crossings.

Gorilla permits should be booked at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak seasons (June-September and December-February). We recommend booking as early as possible as permits are limited and in high demand.

The dry seasons (June-September and December-February) are ideal for most activities. However, we can customize tours year-round based on your interests. For Great Migration, timing varies by location. We'll advise on the best months for your specific combination.
