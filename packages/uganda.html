Why Choose Uganda?

Discover what makes Uganda the Pearl of Africa

Mountain Gorillas

Home to over half the world's mountain gorillas. Experience intimate encounters with these gentle giants in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Big Five Wildlife

Encounter lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, and rhinos in Queen Elizabeth and Murchison Falls National Parks.

Source of the Nile

Visit the source of the world's longest river at Jinja and experience the powerful Murchison Falls on the White Nile.

Best Time to Visit Uganda

Uganda offers excellent safari opportunities year-round, but certain seasons provide optimal experiences for different activities.

Dry Season (June - September)

Ideal for gorilla trekking and game drives. Easier hiking conditions and better wildlife viewing with animals gathering around water sources.

Green Season (March - May, Oct - Nov)

Lush landscapes, fewer crowds, and lower prices. Perfect for photography and birdwatching with over 1,000 species present.
