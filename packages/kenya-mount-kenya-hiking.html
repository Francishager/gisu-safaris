Nairobi to Sirimon Gate - Old Moses Camp

Day 1: The Journey Begins

Early morning pick-up from your Nairobi hotel. Drive through Central Kenya's scenic landscapes to Sirimon Gate (2,650m). After park formalities and equipment check, begin your ascent through the dense montane forest.

Day 2
Alpine Zone

Old Moses to Shipton Camp

Continue through the bamboo and heath zone into the spectacular moorland. Cross the Mackinder Valley with its unique giant groundsels and lobelias. The landscape becomes increasingly dramatic as you approach the alpine zone. Pass several tarns (mountain lakes) and enjoy magnificent views of the main peaks. Arrive at Shipton Camp (4,200m), perfectly positioned for acclimatization.

Acclimatization: Today is crucial for altitude adjustment. Stay hydrated and inform guides of any discomfort.

Day 3
Summit Day

Summit Attempt - Point Lenana (4,985m)

Early pre-dawn start (2:30 AM) for the summit attempt. Navigate through scree slopes and rocky terrain in darkness. Reach Point Lenana (4,985m) at sunrise for spectacular 360-degree views and perfect photo opportunities. On clear days, see Kilimanjaro in the distance! Descend via different route to Austrian Hut for rest, then continue down to Chogoria roadhead.

Summit Day: The most challenging day requiring mental and physical endurance. Weather conditions can change rapidly.
All Meals
Mountain Lodge

Day 4
Descent

Chogoria Route Descent & Lake Ellis

Descend through the spectacular Chogoria route, often considered the most beautiful route on Mount Kenya. Pass pristine mountain lakes including Lake Ellis and Lake Michaelson. Walk through the unique bamboo forest and giant heather zone. The descent offers completely different scenery from your ascent route, showcasing Mount Kenya's incredible biodiversity.

Return

Final Descent & Return to Nairobi

Complete your descent through the montane forest to Chogoria Gate. Celebrate your achievement with your team and guides. Drive back to Nairobi with stops for lunch and souvenir shopping. Arrive in Nairobi by evening with unforgettable memories of conquering Africa's second-highest peak and experiencing some of the continent's most spectacular mountain scenery.
