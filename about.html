About Gisu Safaris

Your trusted partner for extraordinary East African wildlife adventures since 2012

Our Story

Founded in 2012 by passionate conservationists and wildlife experts, Gisu Safaris began with a simple mission: to share the incredible beauty and diversity of East Africa with the world while supporting local communities and conservation efforts.

What started as a small family business has grown into East Africa's premier safari company, trusted by travelers from over 50 countries. Our name "Gisu" comes from the local Bagisu people of Uganda, reflecting our deep roots and commitment to authentic cultural experiences.

Today, we operate across four countries - Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda - offering everything from intimate gorilla encounters to epic Great Migration safaris. Every journey we craft is designed to create lasting memories while contributing to wildlife conservation and community development.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide every safari we create

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading sustainable safari company, inspiring conservation and creating unforgettable experiences that benefit wildlife, communities, and travelers alike.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional safari experiences that showcase East Africa's natural wonders while supporting conservation efforts and empowering local communities through responsible tourism.

Our Values

Sustainability, authenticity, excellence, and respect for wildlife and local cultures. We believe in creating positive impact through every journey we organize.
